# Robotics & Embodied AI

## Current State

Robotics is experiencing a renaissance driven by foundation models and massive investment. Humanoid robots (Figure, Tesla Optimus, 1X, Unitree) are advancing from demos to early deployments. The key enabler: vision-language-action (VLA) models that allow robots to understand natural language instructions and generalize across tasks.

Self-driving continues with Waymo expanding, Tesla FSD improving, and Chinese players (Baidu Apollo, etc.) scaling. Drone autonomy is advancing rapidly. Industrial robotics is being transformed by AI-powered flexibility (Covariant, now part of Amazon).

Key challenge: sim-to-real transfer, hardware costs, and the long tail of real-world situations. But progress in 2025-2026 has been dramatic.
