# AI in Enterprise

## Current State

Enterprise AI adoption is in the "deployment at scale" phase. Most large enterprises have moved past POC/experimentation and are deploying AI in production workflows. The dominant patterns: customer service automation, internal knowledge/search, document processing, code assistance, and data analysis.

The market is splitting between horizontal platforms (Microsoft Copilot for M365, Google Workspace AI, Salesforce Einstein) and vertical AI solutions (healthcare, legal, finance, manufacturing). Vertical solutions often command higher margins due to domain expertise requirements.

Key challenges: ROI measurement, data privacy/governance, change management, and the "last mile" of integrating AI into existing workflows. The consulting/services layer (Accenture, Deloitte, McKinsey AI practices) is booming.
