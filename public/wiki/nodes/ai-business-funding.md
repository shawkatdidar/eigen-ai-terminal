# AI Business & Funding

## Current State

AI remains the dominant category for venture funding, with capital deployment reaching new highs in March 2026. Frontier labs command massive valuations: OpenAI ($150B+), Anthropic ($60B+), xAI ($50B+), while the developer tools layer is catching up (Cursor reportedly at $50B). Europe is asserting itself with AMI Labs (LeCun) raising $1.03B seed at $3.5B — the continent's largest. Infrastructure (Nexthop $500M, Andromeda $60M) and AI security (Xbow $120M unicorn) are the highest-conviction investment categories.

Key dynamics: robotics funding surged ($1.2B+ in one week across 4 startups), Morgan Stanley is publicly warning of an imminent "massive AI breakthrough," and Anthropic launched a $100M Claude Partner Network. The "wrapper" criticism has evolved — successful AI companies now embed AI deeply into workflows with clear defensibility.

Enterprise AI spend is growing rapidly with buyers becoming more sophisticated. The AI services and partner ecosystem markets are expanding as enterprises need help deploying and integrating.
