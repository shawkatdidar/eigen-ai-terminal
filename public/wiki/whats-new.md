## Entries

### 2026-04-06 | new
**Developing Trends now use plain language** — The trends section dropped internal jargon. "Convergence Detector" is now "Developing Trends" with confidence levels explained in plain English. Say "show trends" to see what's forming.

### 2026-04-06 | tip
**Try "dig deeper" on any signal** — Every signal in your morning brief has a causal chain behind it. Say "dig deeper on [topic]" to see what it causes downstream — which markets it affects, what it enables, and what it blocks.

### 2026-04-06 | tip
**Filter by what you're building** — Say "update my context" anytime to change what Eigen filters for. The more specific you are ("I'm building a RAG pipeline for legal docs"), the sharper your morning briefs get.
